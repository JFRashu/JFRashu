Hi! I'm **Jannatul Farzana Rashumoni**, a 3rd year **Computer Science & Engineering** student at **Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET)**, Bangladesh 🇧🇩

I'm passionate about **building software** and **intelligent systems** — from full-stack web applications to machine learning experiments. I love getting my hands dirty with real datasets, training models, and shipping products that actually work.

My interests span across **Artificial Intelligence**, **Machine Learning**, and **Computer Vision**. I enjoy exploring new architectures, experimenting on Kaggle, and connecting research ideas to practical applications.
